# Privacy Policy for Instant Rate Converter

**Last updated: May 4, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in Instant Rate Converter.

## 1. Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. No account registration is required to use the app.

## 2. Data Collection

The app does not collect any personal data such as your name, email address, or location.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The app uses [ExchangeRate-API](https://www.exchangerate-api.com/) to fetch real-time currency exchange rates. When the app requests updated rates, your IP address may be visible to the API provider as part of standard technical communication. No other personal data is shared with any third party.

## 4. Local Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us:

- **Exchange rates** – the latest fetched rates are cached to allow offline use.
- **Selected currencies** – your active currency list is saved so it persists between sessions.
- **App preferences** – your chosen language and theme (light/dark) are saved locally.

This data remains solely on your device and is not accessible to us.

## 5. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this document.
